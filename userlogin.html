About Aqua Insights

Aqua Insights is a next-generation water management platform leveraging AI, IoT, and data analytics to optimize urban water distribution and provide actionable insights to users and city administrators.

Key Features

  • AI-driven monitoring of water supply and consumption.
  • Real-time notifications for leakages and maintenance needs.
  • Smart scheduling system to manage water distribution efficiently.
  • User-driven feedback system for improved water services.

How It Works

Aqua Insights integrates IoT sensors and AI to monitor real-time water data, predict demand, and alert users about critical water supply conditions.
